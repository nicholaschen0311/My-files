## PART 1 — Slides 1–11 (2 min 30 sec)

**[Slide 1]** Good morning — we are SKY LINK, a micro-satellite system connecting every corner of the world to the future.

**[Slide 2]** We'll cover four parts: concept, products and services, marketing strategy, and budget outlook.

**[Slide 3]** Part One — the origin of SKY LINK.

**[Slide 4]** Internet is essential infrastructure, not a luxury — yet hundreds of millions still can't access it. SKY LINK is here to change that.

**[Slide 5]** Four critical gaps: remote areas with no infrastructure, maritime vessels with poor signal, flights without stable internet, and disaster zones where networks collapse.

**[Slide 6]** Our solution: low-orbit micro-satellites delivering global coverage, real-time connection, disaster communications, and digital equity.

**[Slide 7]** Compared to Starlink — SKY LINK is more affordable, Asia-focused, uses lighter satellites with lower equipment costs, and equal disaster-response capability.

**[Slide 8]** Part Two — our products and services.

**[Slide 9]** Three customer segments: B2C — individuals and outdoor adventurers; B2B — airlines, shipping, and logistics; B2G — disaster relief, defense, and rural education.

**[Slide 10]** Three phases: deploy first satellites and cover Asia → expand constellation and add enterprise services → global network with AI management and Space IoT.

**[Slide 11]** Marketing flywheel: awareness → trial → conversion → retention → advocacy. Without SKY LINK: blackout and no rural internet. With SKY LINK: instant rescue and global connection.
